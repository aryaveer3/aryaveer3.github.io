    <h1 class="heading">Computer Science</h1>
    <p class="content">WHAT IS COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING ?
Computer science engineering (CSE) is one of the
 popular courses among engineering aspirants which 
focuses on the basic elements of computer programming and
 networking. Students pursuing computer science courses will gain
 knowledge of design, implementation and management of information 
system of both hardware and software. Going by the name,
 CSE course deals primarily with the theory of computation
 and design of computational systems. The course is offered 
across the globe in technical institutions at undergraduate as
 well as postgraduate levels awarding B.Tech and M.tech degrees, 
respectively.</p>
